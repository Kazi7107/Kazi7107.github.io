Keyboard Access

This site is fully navigable without a mouse. Here's how:

Key(s) Action
TabMove forward through links and navigation items
Shift + TabMove backward through links and navigation items
EnterActivate the focused link or button
← / → in navMove between nav items
Alt + HJump directly to the Home page
Alt + RJump directly to Things to Read
Alt + PJump directly to Photography
EscClose this help panel

The keyboard support on this site is especially designed to achieve two usability principles: visibility of system status and flexibility and efficiency of use. A focus ring is always shown on the active element so you always know exactly where you are, satisfying visibility of system status. Arrow-key navigation and access-key shortcuts let experienced keyboard users move faster, while beginners only need Tab and Enter, satisfying flexibility and efficiency of use.

About

Photography is how I slow down. I started planning trips around locations I wanted to photograph: places where the landscape does something dramatic and where getting the shot means actually being there at the right moment. It's equal parts travel, patience, and luck.

Banff, Alberta

October in the Rockies

The Rockies in October sit in a golden-hour glow for most of the afternoon, and the lakes reflect colours that feel almost unreal. I spent three days there waking up before sunrise and staying out until the last light disappeared behind the peaks.
